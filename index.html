Introducing Nemesis Prime

From completing simple challenges, to doing the impossible; Nemesis Prime does it all. FIRST Tech Challenge Team 1 4020 NEMESIS PRIME was formed on November 28, 2017. FIRST is a program that gets students excited about STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Math) by coming up with intense technical challenges every year. This year Nemesis Prime aspires to make the best robot to the teams ability in order to succeed in this years challenge. Nemesis Prime looks forward to competing in this years FIRST Tech Challenge!
